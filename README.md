# Ultimate Timer Window for SAT Practice!
A chrome extension, perfect to use on the SAT official practice site/question bank <br><br>
Easy, Medium, and Hard buttons start a 1:00, 1:30, or 2:00 timer <br><br>
Summary buttons lets you know the amount of time spent overall and number of questions answered, as well as the average time spent for easy, medium, and hard questions respectively. <br><br>
If you need a timer that sits in the corner of your screen without needing to open an external tab or look down on your phone, this is your go-to!
